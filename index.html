Welcome to the Molecular Ecology Lab (MEL) at the Environmental Lab Section of Delaware’s Department of Natural Resources and Environmental Control (DNREC).

We are focused on using molecular techniques to gain a better understanding of the environment around us. Our approach is broad, using field, molecular techniques, statistics, and code to examine microbial communities in the waters of Delaware.
